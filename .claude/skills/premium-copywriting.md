# Premium Landing Page Copy Skill

Write copy like a serious startup, not a generic AI tool.

## Voice
- Clear
- Confident
- Minimal
- Human
- High-conviction
- Not overhyped

## Avoid
- “Revolutionary”
- “Game-changing”
- “Unlock your potential”
- “Powered by cutting-edge AI”
- Generic SaaS buzzwords

## Prefer
- Short punchy headlines.
- Concrete benefit.
- Specific user pain.
- Clear product explanation.

## ReelAI Examples
Bad:
“Unlock the future of AI-powered learning.”

Good:
“Turn any topic into an endless feed of useful clips.”

Bad:
“Revolutionize the way you consume educational content.”

Good:
“Stop searching through hour-long videos. ReelAI finds the moments that matter.”
